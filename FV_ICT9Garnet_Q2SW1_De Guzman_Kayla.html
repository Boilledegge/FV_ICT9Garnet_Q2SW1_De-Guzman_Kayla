Different Dog Breeds

BELGIAN MALINOIS

Belgian Malinois are known for their box figure and string bodies, they are elegant yet bulky. Fit feirce dogs yet at the same time, Family oriented. They are famous for protection. Some owners use them as guard dogs while others like the law enforcement train them to detect bombs, drugs, etc.


GOLDEN RETRIEVER

Golden retrievers are known for their friendly and playful personalities. They get along with other people and pets easily and they tend to be obedient to their owners. Golden retreivers are huge but gentle but at the same time protective of their owners. This makes many people love them.


SIBERIAN HUSKY

Siberian Huskies are known to be friendly and intelligent. They tend to be dependent on their owners. Huskies are sociable and is loyal to its family. Huskies might seem to be stubborn but its playful and loving personality is what makes its owners adore them.
